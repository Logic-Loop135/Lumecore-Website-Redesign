Our Story

Luxury Meets Science

LUMECOR Fragrance & Decor is a company based in Atlanta, GA. We believe that your home environment is a direct reflection of your well-being, and our mission is to elevate that space with artisanal precision.

Our luxuriously healthy candles were created by a biologist who has spent a lifetime finding ways to promote the health and welfare of humanity. This scientific background ensures that every scent we curate is as mindful of your health as it is of your aesthetic.

"Finding the intersection of biological wellness and high-end fragrance curation."
